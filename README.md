# SingleBit | Mobile App Client Onboarding Playbook

**Type:** Business Analysis / Process Documentation  
**Domain:** Founder's Office | Mobile App Agency  
**Version:** 1.0 | April 2026  
**Status:** Completed

---

## Overview

This playbook is an end-to-end operational document designed for a mobile app agency 
(SingleBit) to standardize how a founder is taken from their first email to a 
live app on the App Store and Play Store.

It defines a 10-stage client lifecycle with stage gates, ownership, tools, 
inputs, and outputs at every step — ensuring consistent quality, predictable 
client experience, and zero gaps in delivery.

---

## Problem Statement

Agencies working with early-stage founders often face inconsistent onboarding, 
unclear handovers, and scope creep due to undocumented processes. This playbook 
solves that by creating a single source of truth for every team member involved 
in a project.

---

## Scope

- Platform: iOS, Android, Cross-platform Mobile Apps  
- Engagement Model: Fixed-price MVP build + optional post-launch retainer

## Key Deliverables Documented

- Qualification checklist with 5 scoring signals
- Discovery call question framework
- Scope document structure with IN / OUT / NICE feature tagging
- Sprint breakdown across 4 sprints (13 weeks standard)
- UAT guide and bug log protocol (P1 / P2 / P3 severity)
- Handoff package checklist covering code, design, credentials, and documentation
- Communication cadence across Slack, Notion, Google Meet, and Loom
